# Health & Longevity

**Evidence-informed skills for building strength, mobility, and lasting health.** — built in-house by [Skill&nbsp;Me](https://skillme.dev).

A curated collection of skills covering nutrition, strength training, lab literacy, injury prevention, longevity protocols, and daily mobility. Each skill provides structured, opinionated guidance grounded in current evidence. General wellness education only — not a substitute for professional medical advice.

⭐ **If this is useful, star the repo** — it's how we gauge what to build next.

## Install

- **From the catalog:** [skillme.dev/pack/health-and-longevity](https://skillme.dev/pack/health-and-longevity) — install the whole pack into Claude in one step.
- **With the skills CLI:** `npx skills add aouellets/health-and-longevity`
- **Manually:** copy any `skills/<slug>/SKILL.md` into your Claude skills directory.

## Skills in this pack

- **[Nutrition Planner](skills/nutrition-planner/SKILL.md)** — Build a sustainable nutrition plan around protein targets, whole foods, and long-term adherence.
- **[Strength Training Plan](skills/strength-training-plan/SKILL.md)** — Design a progressive overload strength program matched to the user's training age, schedule, and equipment.
- **[Bloodwork Explainer](skills/bloodwork-explainer/SKILL.md)** — Explains common lab panels in plain language, typical reference ranges, and what questions to bring to a doctor.
- **[Injury Prehab](skills/injury-prehab/SKILL.md)** — Designs prehab and mobility work to reduce injury risk at common problem areas: shoulders, knees, lower back, and hips.
- **[Longevity Protocol](skills/longevity-protocol/SKILL.md)** — Assembles an evidence-informed longevity routine spanning sleep, zone 2 cardio, strength, and diet.
- **[Mobility Routine](skills/mobility-routine/SKILL.md)** — Builds a daily mobility and flexibility routine for desk workers targeting hips, thoracic spine, shoulders, and ankles.
- **[Fitness Program](skills/fitness-program/SKILL.md)** — Progressive-overload training with sensible volume, deload weeks, and form-first guidance.
- **[Sleep Optimizer](skills/sleep-optimizer/SKILL.md)** — Creates personalized sleep protocols based on chronotype, environment, and recovery goals.
